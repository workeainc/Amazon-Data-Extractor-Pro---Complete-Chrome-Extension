Step 1: Find the Extension Icon

Look for the orange circle icon with a white "P" in your browser toolbar (top right area).

Step 2: Click the Extension Icon

Click on the orange extension icon to open the popup window.

Step 3: If Icon is Hidden

If you don't see the orange icon:

  • Look for the puzzle piece icon (extensions menu)
  • Click it and find "Amazon Data Extractor Pro"
  • Click the pin icon to pin it to toolbar
  • Then click the orange icon

Step 4: Access Your Seller Data

In the popup window:

  • Look for "Products" and "Sellers" tabs
  • Click the "Sellers" tab
  • You should see your 9 extracted sellers

Troubleshooting:

If popup still doesn't open:

  • Go to chrome://extensions/
  • Make sure extension is enabled
  • Click reload button on the extension
  • Refresh the Amazon page (F5)
  • Try clicking the extension icon again
